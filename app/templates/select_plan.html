Frequently Asked Questions

How does WhatsApp integration work?

Customers can browse your entire Shopify catalog, add items to cart, and complete purchases directly through WhatsApp chat. No app downloads required!

What counts as a WhatsApp message?

Both incoming (from customers) and outgoing (bot responses) messages count toward your monthly limit. Product images and cart updates are included.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can change your plan anytime. Upgrades are immediate, downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

How is real-time sync powered?

We use Shopify's latest GraphQL API with webhooks to ensure your WhatsApp catalog is always up-to-date with inventory, prices, and product changes.
